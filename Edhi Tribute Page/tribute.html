Abdul Sattar Edhi

The Hero Pakistan Needed

Edhi took in and raised the children who were abandoned by their parents.

Here's a time line of Edhi's life:

  • 1st Jan 1928: Born in Gujrat, India.
  • 8th July 2016: Dies at the age of 88.

    • "We have lost a great servant of humanity. 
He was the real manifestation of love for those who were socially vulnerable,
impoverished, helpless and poor."
    -Pakistani Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif
